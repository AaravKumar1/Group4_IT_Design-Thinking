About Applovo

Applovo was not always the multinational corporation it is today. In fact, its origins are quite humble, much like most tech startups. It was founded in a small studio apartment near the future Electronics City in Bengaluru. Its founders were 5 schoolmates-Aarav Kumar, Garv Jhunjhunwala, Pritesh Lahiri, Orion Mukhoty, Shaurya Lohia and Medansh More.

One thing led to another, and Applovo grew from a company founded by newly-graduated students into an MNC with its headquarters in Electronics City.

Today, Applovo is one of the biggest IT companies, and has offices in 50 countries. It is known for niche, quality products.
